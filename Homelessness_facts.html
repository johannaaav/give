Homelessness Facts

Here are quick facts about homelessness in Atlanta.

1) About 10,000 people are homeless in Atlanta in any given night
2) Atlanta is the poorest city in the U.S. for children - more children in Atlanta live in poverty than in any other city.
3) 40% of homeless men are veterans.
4) While about 6 percent of the general population suffers from severe mental illness, 20 to 25 percent of the homeless suffer from severe mental illness.
5) 90 percent of homeless women are victims of severe physical or sexual abuse, and escaping that abuse is a leading cause of their homelessness.
6) Many factors can contribute to a person becoming homeless: • Poverty • Lack of affordable housing • Job loss • Lack of health care • Mental illness • Substance abuse • Domestic violence
