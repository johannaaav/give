Homeless Shelter Checklist: What to Bring

Below is a checklist of suggessted donations for what homeless shelters need most.

1) Clothes and Shoes. Especially during the winter, clothes are always needed. Socks, underwear (both men and womens), and gloves (preferably waterproof) are always a big help.
2) Personal Care Items. From toothpaste to feminine care products to soap and lotion, shelters need toiletries. Deordorant, wipes, and diapers are other good donations.
3) Bed sheets, towels, and homeware. These go towards helping people who stay overnight
4) Retail and Tech. Small things like gift cards to common stores, pre-paid cell phones, portable radios, cinema tickets, journals, portable chargers, and headphones can make a homeless persons day, especially considering that many homeless people are children or teenagers in poverty.
5) Donations. The most straight-forward way to make an impact.
