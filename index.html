Who we are

Hello! Welcome to Give, a website created to give others an opportunity to give back to their communities. As three young women, we want to use our knowledge of computer science to make a small but significant impact. With our skills gained from the Girls Who Code program, we were able to produce an aid for anyone who wants to make a positive change in the world.
