Orphanage/Foster Home Checklist

Below is a checklist or suggested ways to help within your community.

1) Become a court-appointed special advocate (CASA) for children in foster care.
2) Volunteer your time at a foster care center.
3) Donate funds.
4) Mentor a child in foster care (Big Brothers Big Sisters, etc).
5) Donate materials like back to school supplies, toys, etc.
